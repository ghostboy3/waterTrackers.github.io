About WaterTrackers

In recent years, more floods have killed residents in North America than average, why? Well, many residents are not prepared to be safe during natural disasters. WaterTrackers is a platform that helps residents in designated areas to be ready for the next flood. Detailed maps will allow users to see if they are in areas with a high risk of a natural disaster. Detailed checklists will prevent users from forgetting small details when packing and be fully equipped. Although we are experts, we will help you be alert with the most relevant information. Precautions You can Take

Before the Flood

  •  Put weather protectant seals around windows and doors
  •  Do not store important documents on lower floors
  •  Prepare an emergency kit
  •  Make an emergency plan with all the members in your household

During the Flood

  •  Evacuate if told to and move to higher grounds
  •  Carefully switch off the electricity only if the fuse box is dry
  •  DO NOT swim/drive around in the waters
  •  STAY AWAY from bridges over water and bodies of water
  •  Store important items in waterproof containers
  •  Listen to authorities and follow given pathways to safe lands
  •  Take your emergency kit with you

After the Flood

  •  Only return once authorities have said it is safe to do so
  •  Only enter your home if the power switch was turned off prior to the flood
  •  Make sure the building is structurally safe
  •  Do not try to switch on any electric items
